## üéØ Quick Testing Steps

1. **Start both servers** (backend and frontend)
2. **Go to Admin Panel**: `http://localhost:3000/admin`
3. **Add 2-3 projects** using sample data above
4. **Add 2-3 clients** using sample data above
5. **Go to Landing Page**: `http://localhost:3000`
6. **Verify projects appear** in "Our Projects" section
7. **Verify clients appear** in "Happy Clients" section
8. **Test contact form** with sample contact data
9. **Test newsletter** with sample email
10. **Go back to Admin Panel** and verify contact messages and subscribers appear

## üí° Tips
